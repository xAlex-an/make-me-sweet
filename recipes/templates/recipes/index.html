FAQ

Everything you need to know about baking and our recipes

Can I substitute ingredients in your recipes?

Yes, many ingredients can be substituted! However, baking is a science, so some substitutions work better than others. For butter, you can use margarine or coconut oil. For eggs, try applesauce or flax eggs.

How long do your baked goods stay fresh?

Most of our baked goods stay fresh for 2-3 days when stored properly at room temperature in an airtight container. Cakes and cupcakes can last up to 5 days in the refrigerator.

Why is oven temperature so important?

Oven temperature is crucial for proper rising, texture, and cooking time. We recommend using an oven thermometer to ensure accuracy, as many ovens run hot or cold.

Do you have vegan or gluten-free options?

Absolutely! We regularly feature vegan and gluten-free recipes. Look for the special tags on our recipe cards. We use alternatives like almond flour and plant-based ingredients.

What basic equipment do I need to start baking?

Start with the basics: mixing bowls, measuring cups and spoons, a hand or stand mixer, baking sheets, and cake pans. A kitchen scale is highly recommended for accuracy.

Can I ask questions about your recipes?

Of course! We love hearing from our baking community. Leave a comment on any recipe, and we'll do our best to help. You can also follow us on Instagram for daily tips.
